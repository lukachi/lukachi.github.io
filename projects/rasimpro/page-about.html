I’m Successful IT Delivery Manager, UX-Designer. Multiple products full-cycle management/development & market research.

overview

I deeply understand the development life cycle and the monetization mechanisms in IT industry products: Web, Ecommerce, Social, Education, Software and gaming products. I manage multiple international products with several teams, both in production and in development state. I’m open to new ideas, propositions and cooperation.

I'm a Senior Project/Product Manager & UX Designer with an attention to details. I have 6 years of successful experience in project/product management and more then 10 years in IT Industry. I know and use Waterfall and Agile methodology: Scrum, Kanban. High Jira proficiency, Confluence, Bitbucket, Gitlab etc. I have experience in making weighed data-driven decisions (A/B testing, User Testing, Analytics, Market Research, Competitor Analysis and KPIs). I have a huge experience with projects based on Java and PHP frameworks Laravel and Yii2. Also I very well know Magento 1/2 platform (CE&EE), CMS Opencart, Shopify, Prestashop, Wordpress (+Woocommerce), Joomla, Drupal. I have good manual QA skills. I have great SEO knowledge and marketing skills. I know HTML/CSS, a little JS, PHP and Java. For example I can make some edits/fixes etc.

Besides, I have 4+ years of successful experience in UX design and in the field of web-product, I have extensive knowledge of UX / UI-design and all its trends. Wireframing: Moqups, Axure, Balsamiq, Marvel app. I can make an effective/detailed wireframe/prototype of any complexity. I have experience as Creative Director in IT/Brand fields.

Except IT field, i'm interested in Game Dev, and I've been watching this market: all the news, trends, conferences and releases. And I'm interested in new opportunities in Game Dev too.

My qualities:
Self-developing, responsible, proactive, sociable, creative, enduring, erudite, purposeful and resourceful.

senior social worker
Company: Caritas Internationalis
January 2015 – June 2016
Humanitarian aid:

I worked with internally displaced persons, in the American Caritas project - OFDA, these people suffered in the South-East of Ukraine. Psychological assistance and support for newcomers and socially vulnerable IDPs. Registration of immigrants. Communication and assessment according to various criteria to determine the necessary assistance (financial or other) to the affected family or individual. Housing monitoring. Preparing for the winter. Working trips and business trips to work with immigrants throughout Ukraine, including front-line zones such as Mariupol and the rest of Donetsk region. I worked on the hotline of Caritas Ukraine. Called for the purpose of monitoring or alerts, invitations to those who received a cash grant. Setting up and updating company iPads, as well as other equipment.

I significantly strengthened my skills and knowledge of psychology: behaviorism, physiognomy, and the psychology of manipulation. I came up with an idea and began to engage in information management: notification through social networks about new projects, which significantly increased awareness among the affected population (IDPs) and reduced the same type of calls to the organization’s hotline. This freed up more social workers to more efficiently accept IDPs and not create crowds.

Helped more than hundreds of thousands of victims of the humanitarian crisis and military conflict in Ukraine.
