what is landing
Landing is a professionally designed landing page, wrapped in a nice looking design, the main purpose of which is to turn website visitors into your customers. Each element and one-page block motivates and brings a potential client to the target action, whether it is an application, subscription or purchase. Thus, the maximum effect is achieved on the target audience of your business.
for what
whel landing is needed
— If you want to quickly sell your product or service;
— When you need to quickly check how relevant the idea is for the business;
— When you need to “probe” a niche before starting a business;
— When you have a limited budget and time to start;
— When you do not need a whole online store for your purpose;
— If you need an effective and profitable one-page site.
